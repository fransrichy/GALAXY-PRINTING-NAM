Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

How long does a typical printing project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and size. Simple projects like business cards can be completed in 1-2 days, while larger projects like vehicle wraps may take 3-5 business days. We provide specific timelines during the consultation phase.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! Our talented design team can create custom designs from scratch or refine your existing concepts. We work closely with you to ensure the final design meets your vision and requirements.

What file formats do you accept for printing?

We accept various file formats including PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, and high-resolution JPG/PNG files. For best results, we recommend vector files (AI, EPS) for logos and text, and high-resolution (300 DPI) images for photographs.
